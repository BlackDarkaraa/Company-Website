Redscan announced as finalist in 3 categories at the Computing Security Awards 2018

The Redscan Team

Home > Cyber Security Blog > Redscan announced as finalist in 3 categories at the Computing Security Awards 2018

Last updated on July 15, 2022 at 13:04 PM

Redscan is pleased to announce that it has been shortlisted as a triple finalist at the 2018 Computing Security Awards.

 

• Network Security Solution of the Year – ThreatDetect™
• Cloud Security Solution of the Year – ThreatDetect™
• Pen Testing Solution of the Year – Redscan Pen Testing

Vote for Redscan here!

In the past two years, we have taken home CSA awards for Pen Testing Solution of the Year and Network Security Solution of the Year, and would really appreciate your help to repeat our successes in these categories.

Plus, this year, ThreatDetect™, our Managed Detection & Response service, has also been nominated for the Cloud Security Solution of the Year award, evidence of our growing presence in cloud security monitoring.

Vote for Redscan here!

The speed at which threats are evolving has not abated in the past year, and the need for organisations to put measures in place to proactively defend against these threats has never been greater. Our services are designed to help organisations to improve their security posture and mitigate the risk of suffering breaches.

Voting for the Computing Security Awards 2018 is now open, and we would really appreciate it if you would take the time to vote for us. Winners will be announced at a ceremony in London on 11th October.

Many thanks for your support.
